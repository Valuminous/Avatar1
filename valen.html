More about me

I have loved animals ever since I was a little girl, and that's the reason why I became a vet.

I love my job especially since I get to travel a lot.

I am originally from France but, at the moment, I live in Nigeria where can be found numerous endangered animal and plant species, including the three-cusped pangolin, the colobus monkey and the Congo grey parrot.
